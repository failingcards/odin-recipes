BBQ Chicken Pizza

Overview

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

Servings: 8

Yield: 1 (12-inch) pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup spicy barbecue sauce
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 °F (175 °C).
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbecue sauce evenly over the crust.
  3. Top with cubed chicken, pepperoncini peppers, chopped red onion, and cilantro.
  4. Sprinkle shredded Colby-Jack cheese over the toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories: 385  |  Fat: 16 g  |  Carbs: 38 g  |  Protein: 23 g

Notes

This BBQ chicken pizza features a flavorful blend of spicy sauce, zesty peppers, onion, and fresh cilantro—all baked until bubbly and delicious. It’s a fun twist on traditional pizza that’s easy to prepare and perfect for sharing.
