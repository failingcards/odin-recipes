Waffles

Overview

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Servings: 6

Yield: 6 waffles

Updated on: August 14, 2025

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1¾ cups milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat a waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil, mixing to combine. Whisk in sugar, then mix in baking powder, salt, and vanilla—stir just until smooth to avoid overmixing.
  3. Spray the preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray. Pour batter onto the hot iron and cook until golden brown and steaming stops—about 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Notes & Serving Suggestions

Serve with traditional toppings like butter and maple syrup, or try blueberry sauce, nuts (pecans or walnuts), vanilla glaze, Nutella, whipped cream, or fresh berries.

You can make the batter ahead and refrigerate for up to a week—just whisk before using. Leftover waffles can be stored in an airtight container in the fridge for up to three days, or frozen for up to three months and reheated in a toaster or microwave.
